Text Styles

H1 Titles

  • font-weight: bolder;
  • font-size: 2em;
  • font-family: "Young Serif"
  • color: Dk Blue Grey unless the background-color is also Dk Blue Grey, in which case, use White.
  • Uses: Primary headline

H2 Titles

  • font-weight: bolder;
  • font-size: 2em;
  • font-family: "Young Serif"
  • color: Dk Blue Grey unless the background-color is also Dk Blue Grey, in which case, use White.
  • Uses: This is the same as h1, but for secondary headlines.

H3 Titles

  • weight: bolder;
  • size: 1.17em;
  • font-family: "Young Serif";
  • Lt Blue Grey on white.
  • Uses: Just for Project Titles

H4 Titles

  • weight: bolder;
  • size: 1em;
  • font-family: "Montserrat";
  • Navy if on white or Lt Grey background-color, or White if on Dk Blue Grey background-color.
  • Uses:

Brand Voice

Authoritative

Does not apologize or equivocate, & provides information that is actionable.

Straight-forward

No higher than 5th grade vocabulary,follows linear thought, & makes the connections for you.

Upbeat

Emphasizes what is over what is not, never speaks disparagingly, & conveys enthusiasm.

Logo Guidelines:

  • Do not reverse logo.
  • Do not apply colors to logo.
  • Do not rotate any individual part of logo.
  • Do not alter proportions of logo.
  • Do not change arrangement of logo.
  • Do not apply gradients, shadows, or other effects to logo.

Button Styling

Primary Button

HTML:

<button class="btn-primary"> Contact </button>

CSS:

.btn-primary {
background-color: rgb(243, 244, 246);
color: rgb(10, 26, 39);
padding: 10px 20px;
border: 1px solid #0a1a27;
font-family: "Montserrat", sans-serif;
text-transform: uppercase;
font-size: 20px;
cursor: pointer;
}

Secondary Button

HTML:

<button class="btn-secondary"> Stay Updated </button>

CSS:

.btn-secondary {
background-color: rgb(92, 134, 156);
color: rgb(243, 244, 246);

border: 1px solid #0a1a27;
border-radius: 2px;

}
